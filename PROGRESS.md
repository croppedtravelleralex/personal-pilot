## Current Reporting Rule

Progress must be reported across three separate dimensions:

- `runtime alive`
- `build status`
- `verification / acceptance status`

## Not Accepted As Sole Proof

- legacy stage-closeout percentage wording
- historical green-test closure claims
- gateway shell health without upstream
- `verify_proxy` volume without fresh browser-mainline evidence
